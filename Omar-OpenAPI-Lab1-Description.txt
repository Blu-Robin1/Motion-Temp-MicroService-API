This is a motion and temperature monitoring system that receives motion detector and temperature readings from sensor stations deployed in specific regions.

The sensor stations transmit the following batched events:

	Motion Detector: station_id, station_name, reporting_timestamp, readings
	Temperature Detector: station_id, station_name, reporting_timestamp, readings

The readings are stored individually in the system to support the study of wild animal behavior and movement. The temperature data also helps identify how and which animal are most affected by climate conditions.

At peak times, the system receives up to 100 batches per hour, with four readings per batch.

The intended users of this system are hunters, wildlife observers, and ecologists.
